# Corpus Acquisition Log

Last updated: 2026-04-21

This records the URL-chasing sprint for suggested corpora. The target remains a published-original-paper `D` versus `N` object with paper/study IDs, sample size, and an effect/test statistic convertible to a Cohen-d-like scale.

## Normalization Pass Added

After acquisition, the tractable corpora were normalized with:

```bash
.venv/bin/python scripts/build_candidate_d_vs_n.py
.venv/bin/python scripts/analyze_candidate_d_vs_n.py
```

## Direct Publication-Bias Denominator Sprint

The target has now been sharpened from "published D-vs-N slope" to direct
publication bias: a known denominator of conducted, registered, submitted, or
regulatory trials; publication status; and a primary/focal effect with N.

Local output from this sprint:

- `data/derived/publication_bias_direct/direct_publication_bias_d_ready_rows.csv`
- `data/derived/publication_bias_direct/direct_publication_bias_d_ready_rows.parquet`

That D-ready extraction currently has 1,280 raw rows and 982 collapsed
study-level rows. The strict subset of explicit journal-reported result rows is
221 rows: 35 Turner 2022 newer antidepressant rows, 19 Turner 2012
antipsychotic rows, 46 Roest 2015 anxiety-disorder rows, and 121 Amarilyo 2016
rheumatoid-arthritis biologic rows. The broader published-status subset has
1,067 rows, mostly Cipriani plus the new Amarilyo FDA/journal pairs.
